## 3.2 — 🏆 Quick Wins (Easy + high impact — do first, ~2 weeks, <$100)
1. **Reprice to lower-market** (Module 2). *+$1,500/mo, $0, 1 day.* — the single fastest dollar.
2. **Claim & optimize Google Business Profile** (correct category "House/Apartment Cleaning," 20+ photos, hours, booking link). *+$1,000/mo, $0.*
3. **Launch the Recurring Cleaning Plan** (Module 7 tiers). *Seeds the entire MRR target.*
4. **Turn on automated review requests** + mirror Thumbtack proof to Google. *+$400–$900/mo.*
5. **Add online booking (Calendly, free) + card payments (Square/Stripe).** *+$600–$1,500/mo.*

## 3.3 — 🚀 Big Moves (Hard but transformational)
1. **Win 3–5 property-manager / apartment-complex turnover contracts** — recurring, high-volume, counter-seasonal. *+$2,000–$5,000/mo.* (Module 4 targets, Module 6 scripts.)
2. **Install the recurring model + CRM end-to-end** (ZenMaid/Launch27 + recurring billing + auto-reminders) — converts a job business into a sellable, predictable one (the Molly Maid blueprint).
3. **Resolve the identity problem and build one clean brand** — consolidate listings, fix NAP, own the "Whistle Clean San Antonio — interior cleaning" identity so all reputation compounds in one place.

## 3.4 — Total estimated monthly leakage
Summing the capture + model gaps (avoiding double-counting overlapping items): **roughly $8,000–$12,000/mo** of value the business could capture but doesn't today — split about evenly between **"earning demand it doesn't capture"** (pricing, GBP, booking, reviews, identity) and **"selling jobs instead of relationships"** (recurring plans, commercial contracts, CRM). The 90-day plan (Module 5) sequences these so the free/easy wins fund the bigger moves.
