## 7C — One-off job revenue model

- **Average job value (post-repricing):** ~**$160** (blended apt sizes + deep add-ons).
- **Capacity (jobs/week):** solo/family **~15** → **~25** with 1 subcontractor → **~38–40** with 2.
- **Revenue ceiling, one-off only:** ~15 jobs/wk × $160 × 4.3 ≈ **$10,300/mo** — but feast-or-famine, capped at owner-hours.
- **With the recurring model:** a stable recurring base (7B) + one-off on top → higher *and* predictable. This is the entire point of the Molly Maid blueprint (90% recurring).
