# Phase 3 — Bottleneck Analysis & Gap Matrix

**Scoring model**
- **Revenue Impact:** High = $1K+/mo (weight 3) · Medium = $300–$999/mo (weight 2) · Low = <$300/mo (weight 1)
- **Effort to Fix:** Low = 1–3 days (1) · Medium = 1–2 weeks (2) · High = 1+ month (3)
- **Priority Score = (Revenue Impact weight × 2) ÷ Effort.** Higher = do sooner. Max = 6.0.

## 3.2 — 🏆 Quick Wins (top 5: highest impact, lowest effort — do these first)

These five are all **High revenue impact + Low effort** and can be live within **2 weeks** for **<$100 in tooling**:

1. **Claim & fully optimize Google Business Profile** — the highest-ROI action that exists for a local service business. Drives the "near me" map pack. *Est. +$1,000/mo.* Owner: Fabrizio.
2. **Turn on automated review requests** — convert 20 years of happy clients into a flood of Google reviews (text-after-every-job + a one-time blast to past clients). *Est. +$400–$800/mo via ranking + trust.* Owner: Fabrizio + NavaX.

4. **Add card payments (Stripe or Square)** — removes friction and enables auto-charge for the recurring plan. *Est. faster cash + enables MRR.* Owner: Jose.
5. **Launch a Recurring Cleaning Plan** (weekly / biweekly / monthly apartment & office cleaning at locked-in member pricing, card on file) — the single change that converts one-off jobs into MRR. Everything else exists to feed this. *Est. the entire $8K–$12K MRR target.* Owner: Jose + NavaX.

> **Honorable mentions also at Priority 6.0:** finishing & deploying the website (Phase 5) and adding the Spanish/bilingual layer. Both ship inside Weeks 1–2 of the plan.

## 3.3 — The core insight

The gaps cluster into two buckets:

- **Capture gaps** (GBP, booking, reviews, website, bilingual) — Whistle Clean *earns* demand through reputation but lets it leak. Cheap to fix, fast payback.
- **Model gaps** (recurring plans, card-on-file, commercial contracts, CRM) — the business sells **jobs** when it should sell **relationships**. This is where durable MRR comes from.

Fix capture first (Weeks 1–4), install the model second (Weeks 2–12). The 90-day plan in Phase 4 sequences exactly this.
