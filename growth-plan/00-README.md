## How to read the scoring

Because we ran this as **framework + your data**, every audit/benchmark figure is one of:

- **✅ Known** — pulled from the business context you gave me.
- **🟡 Estimate** — my best industry-grounded estimate; replace with real numbers when you have them.
- **🔴 INPUT NEEDED** — a number only Jose/Fabrizio can confirm (e.g. exact review counts, monthly revenue). Fill these in and the scores tighten up.

Nothing here is invented as fact. Where I estimated, it's labeled.

## The one-paragraph version

Whistle Clean has the hardest thing to build — **20 years of reputation and perfect 5-star ratings** — and almost none of the easy things that convert that reputation into predictable revenue: no website that books jobs, no card payments, no recurring plans, no commercial contracts, and a digital presence that's effectively invisible to the ~60% of San Antonio that searches in Spanish. The entire growth plan is about **capturing demand the reputation already earns** and **converting one-off jobs into recurring MRR**. Target: **$8K–$12K/mo recurring within 90 days.**
